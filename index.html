This is the powertrain of a car

The power moves from the engine(where the power is generated)-> To the tra`nsmission (where efficiency is maximized via gearing)-> To the Diff where the power is split and moved to the wheel.

This is the gearing in a 5 speed transmission.

When you push the clutch the layshaft disconnects from the engine. You can them move the shifter to select a gear. The blue and red gears will line up. the clutch will be released and the engine will be reattached sending power down the to the differential.
